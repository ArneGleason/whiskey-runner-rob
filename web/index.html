Whiskey Runner Rob

Rob has entered his post-paycheque entrepreneur era: more invoices, worse ideas, no visible boss, and a motorcycle large enough to frighten a mailbox.

Unfortunately, Rob also invented a clear acrylic riding dome so his two parrots can sit on his shoulders while he tears up and down the road like a man conducting very loud bird-based research.

This morning the dome is caked in dead bugs, road dust, and one suspicious smear Rob insists is "probably blueberry." The parrots have filed a visual obstruction complaint by screaming directly into his ears.

First job: get Rob out of the house, get him on the cruiser, ride north to MacLeod's Hardware-ish, pick up acrylic dome polish, and bring it back to the garage before the birds learn stronger language.

Controls: move with WASD or arrows. Use E, Space, or Enter to interact, mount, dismount, or pick the bike up after Rob loses a disagreement with traffic. M opens the mission browser. R drops the selected item. F uses it. Number keys pick inventory slots. Honking while moving is not helpful, but it is emotionally available.

Good luck. Try not to polish the dome with the parrots still licking it.
